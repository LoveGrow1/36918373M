Your partner's
unseen needs

There might be moments when your partner feels unheard or unsupported, particularly when they are feeling low or upset. She may require more active engagement from you during these times.

Encouragement in Expressing Desires:

Although there's a high level of openness in your relationship, she may need more reassurance that she can always express her desires or concerns without fearing any adverse reaction.

Shared Decision Making:

Your partner may desire more involvement in the decision-making process. It may be crucial for her to feel that the decisions made truly represent both of you.
